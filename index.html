The Arcane Game Board

A Chess Cartesian Robot Designed for UCF Senior Design Summer 2021

Desgined by Lucas Lage, Kayla Freudenberger, Fernando Valdes-Recio, and J Anton Strickland

Custom From the Ground Up

Featuring a bespoke PCB, firmware utilizing the Arduino Framework, a React Web-Application, and a chassis designed and built by our team - the Arcane Game Board brings physical board games into the digital age!
